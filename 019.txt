London hope over Chepkemei  London Marathon organisers are hoping that banned athlete Susan Chepkemei will still take part in this year's race on 17 April.
Chepkemei however took part and finished third in last Sunday's world best 10K race in Puerto Rico.
Chepkemei was suspended from all competition until the end of the year by Athletics Kenya after failing to report to a national training camp.
The camp in Embu was to prepare for the IAAF World Cross Country Championships later this month.
